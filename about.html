About Security Systems & More

Florida's Trusted, Independent Security Partner Since 2010

Our Story

Security Systems & More was founded in West Palm Beach with one goal: give Florida homes and businesses security they can actually rely on — not just a system that's "installed and forgotten."

We're a family-run company, which means we care about our reputation in a way the big national companies never will. Our Managing Director brings over 25 years of hands-on experience in the security industry — from sales and design to installation and ongoing service. That experience shows in every system we build.

Because we're independent, we don't push a single brand or have quotas to meet. We work with the manufacturers that actually fit your situation — UL-listed equipment, code-compliant installations, and honest recommendations. No locked-in contracts. No upsells you don't need.

Who We Serve

We serve commercial and residential clients across all of Florida — from single-camera installs to multi-site enterprise deployments. Our clients include local businesses, national chains, and everyone in between. What they all have in common: they wanted a contractor who would show up, do it right, and still answer the phone when they call.
