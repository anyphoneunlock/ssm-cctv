CCTV Monitoring

Your cameras might be down. You'd never know until it's too late.

Most security camera systems look like they're working — green lights, monitors on — but the moment a camera goes offline, a hard drive fails, or video is lost, you don't find out until you need the footage and get nothing.

Our monitoring service watches the actual system health, not just the video feed. We get alerted within minutes of:

  • Camera offline
  • Video loss
  • Hard drive failure
  • DVR/NVR offline
  • Incorrect time drift
  • Connection loss

We contact you — often before you even notice. That's the difference between security and a false sense of security.

Access Control Systems

Know who's coming and going — and who has access to what.

Access control goes beyond cameras. It's about controlling who can enter your building, your office, your storage area — and keeping a record of it. We install and maintain:

  • Keycard and fob entry systems
  • Biometric entry (fingerprints, facial recognition)
  • Door controllers and electric locks
  • Visitor management systems
  • Remote access and monitoring

Training & Ongoing Support

We don't install and disappear.

A system nobody knows how to use is a system that fails when you need it. We provide comprehensive training so your team actually knows how to operate the system — not just that it exists.

  • Fast response when something goes wrong
