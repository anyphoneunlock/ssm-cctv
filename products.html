Network Video Recorders (NVRs)

The brain of your IP security system.

Our NVRs support the latest video compression formats and integrate with a wide range of IP cameras via ONVIF conformance. Built for reliability, with hot-swap HDD support and RAID storage options for critical installations.

  • Support Ultra 265/H.265/H.264 video formats
  • Third-party IP cameras supported via ONVIF (Profile S, G, T)
  • Up to 12 Megapixel resolution recording
  • Hot-swap HDD with RAID storage scheme
  • Up to 16 SATA HDDs, up to 10TB each
  • Cloud storage and remote upgrade support
📹

IP Cameras

From 2MP to 4K — for every environment and every budget.

High-resolution network cameras with smart features like StarLight+ for low-light performance, Smart IR for even illumination, and wide voltage range for unreliable power conditions. Built for Florida's climate.

  • 2 to 12 Megapixel options (1080P to 4K)
  • Up to 3840×2160 resolution, 4K @ 20fps
  • 2.8mm Fixed to 30x Motorized Lens options
  • Smart IR & StarLight+ for low-light environments
  • 2D/3D DNR noise reduction
  • Ultra 265, H.265, ONVIF compatible
  • IP67 weather resistance
  • PoE (Power over Ethernet) with wide voltage range (±25%)
🔌

PoE Switches

Power and data over a single cable — clean, simple, reliable.

Managed PoE switches designed for surveillance applications. Multiple working modes, port traffic control, and up to 250m transmission distance in surveillance mode — so you can power cameras far from your NVR without signal loss.

  • Multiple working modes for different network needs
  • Port traffic control and secure mode
  • All downlink ports communicate only with uplink ports
  • Up to 250m transmission distance in surveillance mode
  • Buffer optimization for smooth video streaming
📺

HD Over Coax Systems

Upgrade your existing coaxial infrastructure without running new cables.

HD CCTV is an open standard for HD digital video over existing coaxial cable — so you don't need to replace your cable runs. Just swap out the cameras and recorder. Our hybrid DVRs support CVBS analog, AHD, HDCVI, HDTVI, and IP cameras all in one system.

  • Works with existing RG59 and coaxial cable
  • Hybrid DVRs support analog, AHD, HDCVI, HDTVI, and IP
  • Up to 4K resolution over existing cable infrastructure
  • Cost-effective upgrade path for older analog systems
